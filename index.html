The British diet has undergone a transformation in the last half-century. Traditional staples such as eggs, potatoes and butter have gradually given way to more exotic or convenient foods such as aubergines, olive oil and stir-fry packs.

Explore the changes across four decades and hundreds of food and drink categories in this interactive visualisation of newly published open data from the Department for Agriculture, Farming and Rural Affairs, part of 2016 - Year of Great British Food.

Created for the ODI by Kiln.
